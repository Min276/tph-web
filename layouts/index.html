What We Do

Educate

We educate leaders of tomorrow by providing a strong, foundational Computer Science education vital to succeed in today's technological era.

Advocate

We advocate for Computer Science to become a core subject in Myanmar and for it to become more widespread and accessible nationwide.

Empower

We empower students to reach their full potential by providing opportunities to grow personally and intellectually through exploration alongside a quality education.

Innovate

We innovate, spearheading the spread of free, accessible technological education in Myanmar since the launch of our non-profit social enterprise.
