About this Course

These little explorers tend to have complex ideas and don't hesitate to ask questions to figure out how things work. Our courses introduce the fundamental concepts of Computer Science and AI, providing kids a new world to explore with endless possibilities. The lessons also teach them to meaningfully collaborate with others and face challenging tasks with different problem-solving techniques & critical thinking.
